### When You're Ready:

1. **Visit AdSense:**
   - Go to: https://www.google.com/adsense/
   - Click "Get Started"

2. **Sign Up:**
   - Use your Gmail: k8744185@gmail.com
   - Enter your site URL: `https://your-app.onrender.com`
   - Select country: India
   - Accept terms

3. **Get Your Publisher ID:**
   - After signup, you'll see: `ca-pub-XXXXXXXXXXXXXXXX`
   - **IMPORTANT:** Copy this number!

4. **Update Your Site:**
   - Open `.env` file
   - Replace: `GOOGLE_ADS_CLIENT_ID=ca-pub-xxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxx`
   - With your real ID: `GOOGLE_ADS_CLIENT_ID=ca-pub-1234567890123456`
   - Also update in Render environment variables

5. **Deploy Changes:**
   - Commit and push to GitHub
   - Render will auto-deploy
   - Verify ad placeholders appear

6. **Verify Site in AdSense:**
   - Go to AdSense ‚Üí Sites
   - Click "Ready to activate"
   - AdSense will check for ad code
   - **This can take 1-2 days**

7. **Wait for Approval:**
   - Review time: 1-7 days (usually 2-3 days)
   - Check email for updates
   - Don't modify site during review
