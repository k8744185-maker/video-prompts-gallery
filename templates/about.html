About Video Prompts Gallery

We are a free, community-driven platform dedicated to helping filmmakers, content creators, and AI enthusiasts produce stunning AI-generated videos through the power of expertly crafted prompts.

Our Story

Video Prompts Gallery was born from a simple frustration: getting great results from AI video generators is hard. Tools like Runway ML, Pika Labs, Kling AI, and Stable Video Diffusion are incredibly powerful, but without the right prompt, results can be unpredictable — blurry, incoherent, or just plain boring.

We started collecting and refining prompts that consistently produced cinematic, professional-looking results. Word spread, and what began as a personal library grew into a free public resource used by creators around the world.

Today, Video Prompts Gallery is a curated collection spanning dozens of categories — from sweeping nature cinematography to gritty urban noir, from romantic portraiture to breathtaking sci-fi landscapes. Every prompt in our collection has been tested and refined for real results.

Our Mission

We believe that cinematic creativity should not be locked behind expensive software or years of film school training. AI video generation is democratizing visual storytelling — and we want to make sure everyone has access to prompts that actually work.

Our mission is simple: provide the highest quality, most cinematically accurate AI video prompts available, completely free of charge, to anyone who wants them.

What Makes a Great AI Video Prompt?

Not all prompts are created equal. After testing thousands of combinations across multiple AI platforms, we have identified the key elements that separate a mediocre prompt from a stunning cinematic result:

  • Camera Movement: Specifying the exact shot type (dolly, tracking, crane, handheld) dramatically improves output quality.
  • Lighting Conditions: Precise lighting description (golden hour, neon-lit, diffused overcast) anchors the AI's visual understanding.
  • Mood and Atmosphere: Referencing known cinematic styles (Blade Runner, Studio Ghibli, Christopher Nolan) guides tone effectively.
  • Technical Quality Markers: Adding "4K", "anamorphic lens", "shallow depth of field" signals the target render quality.
  • Sensory Details: Sounds, textures, and even temperature cues surprisingly improve visual coherence in generative models.

Supported AI Video Platforms

Our prompts are optimised for and regularly tested against the industry's leading AI video generation platforms:

  • Runway ML (Gen-2 & Gen-3 Alpha): Industry-leading text-to-video model with exceptional motion quality.
  • Pika Labs: Excellent for short, expressive clips with strong stylistic control.
  • Kling AI: Outstanding for complex scenes with multiple moving elements and characters.
  • Stable Video Diffusion: Open-source option ideal for technical users who want full control.
  • Luma Dream Machine: Remarkable for photorealistic scenes and natural motion physics.
