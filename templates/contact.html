Frequently Asked Questions

Are all prompts really free?

Yes, completely. Every prompt in the gallery is available under a Creative Commons Zero (CC0) licence, meaning you can copy, modify, and use them for personal or commercial projects without asking for permission or attribution.

Which AI tools work best with your prompts?

Our prompts are primarily tested on Runway ML Gen-3, Pika Labs, Kling AI, Luma Dream Machine, and Stable Video Diffusion. They also work well on newer tools like Sora (when publicly available) and Haiper. The cinematic language in our prompts is largely tool-agnostic.

How often do you add new prompts?

We aim to add new, tested prompts weekly. You can bookmark the gallery and check back regularly, or follow us for announcements. Each prompt goes through a manual review process before being published to ensure quality.

Can I request a specific type of prompt?

Absolutely. Email us with your request — the category, mood, setting, or type of shot you're looking for — and we'll do our best to create and test a prompt that meets your creative needs and add it to the collection.

Do you offer any paid services?

Currently, everything on Video Prompts Gallery is free. We are exploring a premium tier that would offer exclusive prompts, advanced prompt editing tools, and batch download capabilities. If that interests you, drop us an email to be added to the early access list.
